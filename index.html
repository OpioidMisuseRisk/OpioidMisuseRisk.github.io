Opioid Misuse Risk Tool

An interpretable, data-driven tool to help doctors determine patients' individual risk of misusing opoids

Our Tool

Machine Learning Technology

OMR-Tool harnesses the power of a machine learning algorithm trained on hundreds of thousands of data points.

Personalized Report

Each report includes personalized and ranked risk attributes unique to the patient, as well as a risk percentage and percentile to let doctors know where the patient lies in the overall population distribution.
