The Data

Our tool was trained on data gathered by The National Survey on Drug Use and Health (NSDUH) during the years 2015-2017. NSDUH is a nationwide study providing information on tobacco, alcohol, drug-use, mental health, and other health-related issues in the general population of the US. The study collects data via individual interview from approximately 70,000 teens and adults each year and the results are used to inform various public health programs, policies, and tools like our risk assessment app. For more information on the survey, please visit their website: https://nsduhweb.rti.org/
